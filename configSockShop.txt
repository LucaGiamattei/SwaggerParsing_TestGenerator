********************* POSSIBLE CONFIGURATIONS
**	
**	jsonFile=<name of the file with json names for parsing>
**	testMode=<normalmode,quickmode,pairwisemode,validmode,validmodeP,validinvalidmode,quickboundedmode>
**	priorityMode=<allfailure,onlysevere>
**	logMode=<log,nolog,token>
**	
**	ATT - with "logMode=log" you need to specify:
**		username=<username>
**		password=<password>
**		loginUrl=<api url to forward login request>
**	    - with "logMode=log" you need to specify:
**		token=<authentication token>
**	
**	OSS - when "**" is found the line is ignored
**	    - the order of the parameter is important
**
**

jsonFile=JsonFilesSockShop.txt
testMode=pairwisenvmode
priorityMode=allfailure
logMode=nolog
**username=user
**password=password
**loginUrl=http://localhost:12340/api/v1/users/login
